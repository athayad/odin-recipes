How to Make Indomie

Description

A quick and tasty instant noodle dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Indomie (any flavour)
  • Water
  • Seasoning (comes with the noodles)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add the Indomie noodles and cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Drain most of the water, leaving a little.
  4. Mix in the seasoning packets.
  5. Stir well and serve hot.

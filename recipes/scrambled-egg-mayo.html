How to Make the Best Scrambled Eggs Mayo

Description

Soft scrambled eggs mixed with creamy mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk.
  2. Heat a pan and pour in the eggs.
  3. Stir gently until softly scrambled.
  4. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  5. Mix in mayonnaise.
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Serve warm

How to Make the Best Sushi at Home

Description

Two types of easy sushi - a vegetable maki roll and a spicy tuna roll. Both made super simple and the only fish here is tinned tuna so there's no need to worry if you don't have access to a good fishmonger.

For this recipe you will need a bamboo sushi mat.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Nori Sheets (seaweed)
  • Salmon Fillets
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cucumber
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the sushi rice and let it cool.
  2. Place one nori sheet on the bamboo sushi mat.
  3. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori.
  4. Add salmon, cucumber slices, and a small line of mayonnaise.
  5. Roll everything up tightly.
  6. Slice the roll into pieces.
  7. Serve with soy sauce for dipping.
